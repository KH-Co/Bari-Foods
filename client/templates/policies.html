Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship to all locations across India. International shipping is not available at this time.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on:

  • Order value
  • Delivery location
  • Product weight and dimensions

Free Shipping: Orders above ₹499 qualify for free standard shipping across India.

3. Delivery Time

Estimated delivery times:

  • Metro Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Other Cities: 5-7 business days
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 business days

These are estimated times and may vary based on location and external factors.

4. Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 24-48 hours of placement. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

5. Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order status through our website or the courier partner's website.

6. Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with delivery:

  • Contact us within 7 days of expected delivery date
  • Provide your order number and details
  • We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly

7. Undelivered Packages

If a package is returned to us as undeliverable due to incorrect address or unavailability:

  • We will contact you to arrange redelivery
  • Additional shipping charges may apply for redelivery
  • If redelivery is not possible, a refund will be processed after deducting original shipping costs

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order:

  • Before Shipment: Full refund within 24 hours of order placement
  • After Shipment: Cancellation not possible; please refer to our Return Policy

2. How to Cancel

To cancel your order:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "My Orders" section
  3. Select the order you wish to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Order" and follow the prompts

Alternatively, contact our customer support at +91 9829288937 or barifoods.in@gmail.com

3. Refund Process

Once your cancellation is confirmed:

  • Refund will be initiated within 5-7 business days
  • Refund will be credited to your original payment method
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed

4. Refund Timeline

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 7-10 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: 5-7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer within 7-10 business days (account details required)

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Products marked as non-returnable
  • Opened or used food products (due to hygiene reasons)
  • Products damaged due to misuse
  • Custom or personalized orders

6. Partial Cancellations

For orders with multiple items, you can cancel individual items before shipment. Partial refunds will be processed accordingly.

Return & Exchange Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Return Eligibility

You can return products within 7 days of delivery if:

  • Product is damaged or defective
  • Wrong product was delivered
  • Product quality doesn't match description
  • Product is unopened and in original packaging

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened food products (due to hygiene and safety reasons)
  • Products without original packaging and tags
  • Products damaged due to misuse or mishandling
  • Products beyond the 7-day return window

3. How to Return

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact us within 7 days of delivery at +91 9829288937 or barifoods.in@gmail.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Share photos of the product (if damaged or defective)
  4. Our team will guide you through the return process

4. Return Shipping

  • Defective/Wrong Products: We arrange free pickup or provide a prepaid return label
  • Change of Mind: Customer bears return shipping costs

5. Exchange Policy

Product exchanges are available for:

  • Same product in different size/variant (if applicable)
  • Equal or higher value products

Exchanges are subject to stock availability. If exchange is not possible, a refund will be issued.

6. Refund After Return

Once we receive and inspect the returned product:

  • Refund will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • You will receive a confirmation email
  • Amount will be credited to your original payment method

7. Quality Assurance

All our products undergo strict quality checks before dispatch. However, if you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us immediately with photos
  • We will arrange immediate replacement or refund
  • No questions asked for genuine quality issues
