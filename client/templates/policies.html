Shipping & Delivery Policy

Processing Time

Orders are processed and dispatched within 24–48 hours.

Delivery Timeline

Standard delivery across India takes 3–7 business days.

Tracking

Cancellation, Refund & Return

Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch for a full refund. Once dispatched, cancellations are not accepted due to perishable nature.

Returns

Returns are accepted only for damaged, expired, or incorrect products. You must notify us within 24 hours with photographic evidence.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 working days.
